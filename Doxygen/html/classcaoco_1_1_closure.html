Detailed Description

A closure is a range of tokens representing a singular ast node.

All tokens from front to back are a single ast node with no children. When a closure is a single token, the front and back are the same. Example: 1 + 2 -> Closure #2 is +, •+ is the front and +• is the back. Example: 1(abcd) + 2 -> Closure #2 is (abcd), •(abcd) is the front and (abcd)• is the back.
